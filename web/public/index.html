How to Play

  • The goal: walk the entire PCT before winter catches you — 2,650 miles, Mexico to Canada.
  • No health, only Morale. If your Morale drops below zero, you leave the trail and your hike is over.
  • The winter line chases you up the trail. If it reaches your position, you're snowed off trail. Keep moving.
  • Hike to advance a day. Your daily miles depend on Speed, Energy, Morale, terrain, and pack weight.
  • Encounters are resolved by a stat check (your stat + a d20 vs a difficulty). Choose wisely.
  • Towns let you spend money to restore Morale, Energy, and Snacks, buy gear, or take a zero day.
  • Tramily: recruit fellow hikers for passive bonuses. Gear gives bonuses but wears and breaks.
